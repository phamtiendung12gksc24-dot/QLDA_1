========================================
  HUONG DAN SU DUNG GIO HANG & DAT HANG
========================================

CAC CHUC NANG DA CO SAN:

1. THEM VAO GIO HANG
   - Tu man hinh "Xem san pham"
   - Click button "Them vao gio" tren san pham
   - San pham se duoc them vao gio hang

2. XEM GIO HANG
   - Click button "Gio hang" tu man hinh Admin
   - Xem danh sach san pham da them
   - Tang/giam so luong
   - Xoa san pham
   - Xem tong tien

3. DAT HANG
   - Tu man hinh Gio hang: Click "Dat hang"
   - Hoac tu man hinh Admin: Click "Dat hang"
   - Nhap thong tin nguoi nhan
   - Xac nhan dat hang

QUY TRINH DAY DU:

1. Dang nhap → Man hinh Admin
2. Xem san pham → Chon san pham → Them vao gio
3. Xem gio hang → Kiem tra → Dat hang
4. Nhap thong tin → Xac nhan → Thanh cong!

LUU Y:

✓ Phai dang nhap truoc khi them vao gio
✓ Server phai dang chay (port 3000)
✓ Rebuild app neu co thay doi code
